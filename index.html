QUESTIONS

Please visit our support and blog pages if you are looking for detailed information about upcoming IDOs, recent updates and programs.

If you have suggestions for FAQ, please email to angel@binstarter.io


Where and how to buy $BSR?

You can buy $BSR from PancakeSwap. Here is the link https://pancakeswap.finance/swap#/swap?inputCurrency=0xab287e6 d370c61f105630e656b5468acb4d00423 Also, here is a step-by-step guide on how to do it : https://support.binstarter.io/article/10-how-to-buy-bsr-from-pancakeswap

Who should I contact for a partnership/ recruitment/ IDO proposal?

Please reach us @Angel_PolkaStation on telegram or email us directly at angel@binstarter.io

What is the total supply of $BSR?

Please kindly visit coinmarketcap or check our whitepaper for details. https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/Polkastation/

What is the circulating supply of $BSR?

You can check the circulating supply for $BSR from the link below; http://binstarter.com/api/c_supply.php

How much should I stake for guaranteed allocation?

For guaranteed allocation, you need to stake at least 2,000 $BSR which is equivalent to Tier 2. Please visit our support section for Tier details.

Do I need to perform KYC to particpate in IDO?

No, it is not required.

How can I apply for whitelist?

You can do it from IDO Projects found on this link https://bsr.binstarter.io/ido/projects by clicking Join Whitelist. You may check this link for the step-by-step process: https://support.binstarter.io/article/8-how-to-join-a-whitelist.

Can I join the Whitelist without staking BSR?

Yes! You can try joining the Gleam contest or the FCFS round.

I applied whitelist for the last IDO. Do I have to do it again?

Yes! You are required to apply whitelist every time when you are joining an IDO.

How do I check if I am selected in the lottery or not?

You can check the result in this link, https://bsr.binstarter.io/ido/projects. Click the project you have applied whitelist for and you will see a label "Whitelisted"on the Gleam pool if you are one of the lucky winners.

What's the accepted currency for an IDO? ETH, BNB, USDT or BUSD?

It varies per project. To be guided on which currency the project runs, visit IDO Projects found on this link https://bsr.binstarter.io/ido/projects. The currency is indicated for every project.

Where can I see my allocation?

Navigate thru "My Allocations" tab. Here's a link for your guide. https://bsr.binstarter.io/my-allocations.

I have already purchased the tokens. But, I do not see my tokens.

After buying a token, you need to claim it. Just wait until the claim time begins. Then, claim your token and you will find the tokens in your wallet. You can check your allocations and claim your tokens on "My Allocations" tab on the website. Please refer to this link: https://bsr.binstarter.io/my-allocations.

When is the gleam result announced?

The results of each Gleam contest is announced before the IDO starts.

I have claimed my tokens but, I can't see it. OR I don't see my refund.

Please verify first, whether the token had been sent or not from below explorers before raising it to our support group chat. You just need to paste the address and verify whether the transaction is there or not.
